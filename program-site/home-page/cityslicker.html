MOBILE APP DESIGN

For this project we were tasked with choosing a certain UX problem, and solving it through designing an app to address said problem. We were to design multiple screens to show the uses, and flow of our apps interface. For my app I chose to create a hybrid of: Find My Friends, Yelp, and Waze to create an app that combines a number of different socially essential apps into one hub. The end product is my personal app, City Slicker.
