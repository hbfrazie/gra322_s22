SINGLE PAGE MARKETING SITE DESIGN

For this project we were tasked with either selecting, or creating, a company to design a single page responsive website for. The design was to have 4-5 body sections, with a header and footer as well. I chose to create a consignment clothing company that solely specialized in reselling the hottest trends in streetwear. For it being my first time attempting website design, I thoroughly enjoyed the process and was proud of the final.
